This is a reaction testing game made with HTML, CSS and ONLY 66 LINES of Javascript, no libraries were used.

Once you hit play, a circle or a square shape with a randomly generated color appears in a "random" location.
After clicking the shape, your reaction time will be calculated and displayed on top.
A new shape will appear after a "random" period of time.

The goal of the game is to click the colored shapes as quickly as you can.
